Captain

Captain Steel India Limited is one of the leading producers of TMT bars in India. We began our journey in 2007, by establishing our first manufacturing plant in Kalyaneshwari, West Bengal.The same year, this facility began producing TMT bars using Thermex technology from Germany. Soon after, we set up two new manufacturing units at Bihta, Bihar, and Barjora, West Bengal. This increased our number of units to three and the annual production capacity to 5,25,000 MT per year.
